PhoneStateListener is an Android app that can help to send a mail notice when your phone recieves any sms or incalling.
Use it after your setting sender email address, password, smtp serverhost(eg smtp.163.com), serverport(25 in general), and reciever email address.
The app needs android.permission.READ_CONTACTS to match phone number when sending the mail,
		needs android.permission.INTERNET to send the mails,
		needs android.permission.READ_PHONE_STATE to watch phone state,
		needs android.permission.RECEIVE_SMS to activate when sms comes.
